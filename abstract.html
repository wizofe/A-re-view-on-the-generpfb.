The purpose of this courseworis to provide computational research information on rpfb, a gene in the Mycobacterium Tuberculosis (MTb). It is also known by its ID: RV1009. The approach is going to be methodological, as discussed in Sequence Analysis lectures. Computational Software, Online Databases and Web Services like Protein BLAST,  HHBlits and InterPro are utilised to retrieve the required information in order to generate further insights.
