Results

To discover the conserved protein domains of the protein, I used NCBI's Conserved Domain Database (CDD). The protein is characterised as resuscitation-promoting factor, which is a cell-wall glycosidase that cleaves cell-wall peptidoglycan; it stimulates resuscitation of dormant cells. Three domains are conserved: YabE, Transglycosylas and G5. Similar results can be shown by using the InterPro protein database.
